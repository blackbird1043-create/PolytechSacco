Our Story

50+ Years of Financial Excellence

The Polytech Sacco Society Ltd was inaugurated in 1972 and officially registered under registration No. CS 2046 as a Sacco with the ministry of Cooperative Society Act 490, No 12 in 1997. Since then, our membership has grown by leaps and bounds as we've continuously evolved our by-laws to better serve our members.

Our Mission

To be the preferred financial institution offering high quality diversified products to customers through participation of its customers.

Our Vision

To provide high quality and diversified products to customers through efficient and effective operations.
